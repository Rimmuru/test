Rimurus Menu

Rimurus Menu is built upon the Cheese UI Framework for the 2Take1 Menu API.

Providing a reliable script with frequent updates.

Mainly developed by Rimuru and GhostOne

Features

Feature Description
Visions 700+ filters for GTA
FOV Adjusts your fov
Player Opacity Change your peds opacity. 0% will be fully invisible
Reset Opacity Reset the above back to default (100%)
Become a cop you will be unable to shoot at cops.Army will still shoot at you
Tiny Player Embrace the small King that you are
Reduced Collision Disables collisions for vehicles and some other objects
2k Money Drop Steal peds of their life earnings
Feature Description
